THE QUANTUM MOON

The Quantum Moon is a mysterious moon in a constant state of quantum superposition. It does not have one specific planet that it orbits and instead can be found orbiting each of the planets in the solar system. However, the Quantum Moon sometimes disappears without a trace. To track the location of the Quantum Moon, the Nomai built a Quantum Moon Locator on the south pole of Ember Twin.

NATURAL PHENOMENA

Due to its quantum state, the Quantum Moon cannot be landed on. When attempting to pass through the Quantum Moon's foggy atmosphere, it will disappear and move to orbit a different planet.

Successfully landing on the Quantum Moon will reveal that its surface imitates the planet that it is orbiting. The Quantum Moon's surface has six different variants depending on what it's orbiting, with the sixth location imitating the location the Quantum Moon originates from.
