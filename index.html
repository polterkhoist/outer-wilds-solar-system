ABOUT OUTER WILDS

Outer Wilds is an open-world exploration video game where you play as the newest recruit of the Outer Wilds Ventures, a fledgling space program searching for answers in a strange, constantly evolving solar system. Trapped in an endless time loop, your goal is to explore the many locations in the solar system, investigate ancient ruins, and translate ancient text to solve puzzles and uncover the mysteries and secrets hiding within the solar system. Outer Wilds' solar system consists of six planets, three moons, and one comet, as well as a few other interesting places to be explored. Each planet has unqiue features and natural phenomena that make them distinct from one another. Some planets even have hidden locations that change with the passage of time.
